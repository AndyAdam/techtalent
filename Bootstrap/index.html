What is Bootstrap 4

Bootstrap is a famous, open-source, front-end frameworks of the Web.

From its first version in 2011, a ton of changes and new features have been inserted.

Today is one of the most popular responsive frameworks for the front-end developer.

Responsive Breakpoints

Small devices (landscape phones, 576px+ and up): .col-sm-*


Medium devices (tablets, 768px and up): .col-md-*


Large devices (desktops, 992px and up): .col-lg-*


Extra large devices (large desktops, 1200px and up): .col-xl-*
